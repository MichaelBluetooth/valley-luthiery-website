Custom Builds

Most custom projects require several consultations. I like to see and hear my clients play at least once. Then we can discuss design goals. A player’s desires vary so much that I’ve never made the same guitar twice. As such, every guitar starts with a drawing. The design inevitably requires iterations and several drawings. Once the design is approved, I will begin the build.

Expect 3-6 months from our beginning conversation to delivery.

Prices begin around $3,000 for solid body electrics and around $1,000 for tube amplifiers. Contact me for more accurate pricing based on your needs.
